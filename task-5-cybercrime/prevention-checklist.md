# Stay Safe Online – Identity Theft Prevention Checklist

- Never share personal information such as Aadhaar number, bank details, or passwords online
- Avoid clicking on unknown links or suspicious emails
- Always check website authenticity before entering sensitive data
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication for added security
- Do not share OTPs or verification codes with anyone
- Regularly monitor your bank transactions and account activity
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Be cautious while sharing personal details on social media
- Log out from accounts after using shared or public devices

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline: 1930
