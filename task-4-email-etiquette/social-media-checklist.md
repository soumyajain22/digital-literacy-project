# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## Do's
- Use respectful and polite language
- Keep your profile professional
- Think before posting anything online
- Protect your privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing

## Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
- Avoid spreading fake news or rumors
- Do not share your passwords with anyone
